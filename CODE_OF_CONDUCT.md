# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers via repository issues (for non-sensitive reports) or security contact listed in `SECURITY.md` (for sensitive reports).
